= Addresses
:description: Configure multiple mailing, billing, and shipping addresses, along with their associated phone numbers, within the User Account section
:revdate: 2020-09-11
:page-revdate: {revdate}
:page-author: SUSE Product & Solution Documentation Team
:page-image: https://www.suse.com/assets/img/suse-white-logo-green.svg
:page-product-name: SUSE Manager
:page-product-version: 4.3

The menu:Home[User Account > Addresses] section allows you set your mailing, billing, and shipping addresses, and associated phone numbers.

Click btn:[Fill in this address] or btn:[Edit this address] below the address to be modified or added, make your changes, and click btn:[Update].
