.Procedure: Checking synchronization progress from the {webui}
[role=procedure]
____
. In the {productname} {webui}, navigate to menu:Admin[Setup Wizard] and select the `Products` tab.
  This dialog displays a completion bar for each product when they are being synchronized.

. Alternatively, you can navigate to menu:Software[Manage > Channels], then click the channel associated to the repository.
  Navigate to the `Repositories` tab, then click `Sync` and check `Sync Status`.
____
